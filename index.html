GPA Calculation Notes:
  • GPA is the grade point average for courses studied over a semester, trimester, or all terms within an academic year.
  • Repeat courses are not allowed.
  • Grades are based on the following scale:
    • HD = 5, DI = 4, CR = 3, PA = 2, CP = 1, F = 0
  • The formula used is:

    GPA = \( \frac{\sum (\text{Grade Points} \times \text{Credits})}{\sum (\text{Credits})} \)

  • For cumulative results across multiple semesters or years including repeated courses, use the CGPA Calculator.
