About

Empowering UPNG students with accurate academic tools.

Features & Purpose

The UPNG GPA Calculator is a user-centric solution designed to simplify grade point calculations for students at the University of Papua New Guinea. What was once a tedious manual process is now instant and accurate.

Key Features

  • Integrated Course Search: Autocomplete feature populated with official UPNG course data.
  • Automatic Credit Fetching: Select a course, and the correct credit values are applied instantly.
  • CGPA Repeat Logic: Automatically handles repeated courses by selecting the highest grade achieved, per university policy.
  • Mobile Optimized: A responsive design that works perfectly on phones and tablets.
  • Offline Capability: Built to be lightweight and fast, even in low-connectivity environments.

The calculations performed here align with the official formulas used by the UPNG student system. However, this is an unofficial tool and should be used for personal tracking and verification only.
